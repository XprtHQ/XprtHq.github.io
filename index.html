Who we are

Xprt Tactical is a tactical gaming/military simulation group. We operate with like-minded members to get the best experience while gaming. Members of Xprt Tactical are expected to perform professionally and proficiently while maintaining a sense of realism. Our Members are expected to be active within our social communities. We hold weekly Battles, Training, and regular gaming days. While engaged in an operation, Members are expected to maintain their roles assigned by their superiors. You could probably consider us a military roleplay group.
